Sune Chung Jepsen is a PhD (2019-2022) student at the SDU Software Engineering section. His doctoral project aims to investigate software architectures for production systems in relation to Industry 4.0. The research includes architecture evaluation, functional and non-functional requirements for infrastructure platforms that support smart manufacturing systems, as well as implementing and evaluating actual middleware for the systems. Software architecture and non-functional requirements are the main focus in order to facilitate the integration of smart production in the I4.0 Laboratory and companies.
